Master of Computer Applications (MCA)

The Master of Computer Applications Program (MCA) program offers in-depth knowledge in the fields of Computer Applications. This master degree degree program enables students to learn about advanced Computing, web design, Application development, and computer vision. The curriculum is designed to prepare students for the challenges of the IT industry.
